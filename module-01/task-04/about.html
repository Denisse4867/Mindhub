Mission

To support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practicle skills related to the game soccer, specifically those skills around teams cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athetles through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information

The Northside Youth Soccer League established in 1996 to provide athetles residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL is ran by a smll full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
